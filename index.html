About Us

Bruce Matzkin

For over 20 years Attorney Bruce Matzkin has been representing victims of workplace discrimination, sexual harassment, wrongful termination, retaliation and other wrongful or illegal conduct in the workplace. The Covid pandemic has created new issues for the employment setting, and Atty. Matzkin is here to guide you.

In addition, Atty. Matzkin handles wage issues, mis-classification issues, and workplace injuries.

Atty. Matzkin can advise you on employment contracts, non- competition agreements and severance agreements, including negotiating terms of agreements on your behalf.

There is no fee for your initial consultation with Atty. Matzkin. After learning about your situation, he will give his candid opinion and will explain how he can help.
